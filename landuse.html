Land Use Classification

To map out the different land classifications in the Area of Utrecht, I used data from the satellite Sentinel-2, which contains a series of two Earth observation satellites of the European Space Agency (ESA) and is part of the Copernicus programme. This satellite focuses on providing vegetation information, depending on the wave signal they receive back


I ended up creating an unsupervised pixel-based map.

  • Unsupervised= The software identifies natural groupings (clusters) in the data using algorithms like K-means or ISODATA.
